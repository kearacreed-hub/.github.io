About Me

I design learning experiences that spark curiosity and bring a little magic back into learning! With over five years in digital learning, I specialise in transforming complex training content into interactive, human-centred journeys that inspire real behaviour change.

Using tools like Articulate, Synthesia, Biteable and the Adobe Creative Cloud, I build immersive modules that blend motion, narrative, and accessibility. From scenario-based learning and branching simulations to video-driven storytelling, I focus on creating experiences learners remember and apply.

I've collaborated with organisations such as the NHS Wales, Maestro and EDF to craft scalable, multimedia-rich courses and streamline LMS delivery- always with the goal of making learning more engaging, intuitive, and impactful.
